Wash hands

Often and thorough hand wash. Use hand sanitizer or disinfecting wipes if hand wash is not possible

Limit physical contact

Avoid shaking hands, kisses on the cheek and hugs

Show proper coughing etiquette

Don't cough or sneeze on others. Use handkerchiefs or the bend in your elbow.

Stay at home if sick

People who show signs of severe symptoms should contact their doctor over the phone and avoid going outside as much as possible. Follow your local goverments advice.

Be mindful of your surroundings

Show appropiate behaviour in areas with larger numbers of people, public transport, visting the elderly or sick.
